About 4 Simple Technique

We're passionate about creating simple, effective online tools that make your digital life easier. Learn more about our mission, our team, and the values that drive everything we do.

Our Story

Founded in 2022, 4 Simple Technique began with a simple idea: to create web tools that are truly accessible to everyone. We noticed that many online utilities were either too complex, loaded with ads, or required personal information to use.

Our founder, Oussama Achouri, started building simple web utilities for his own use and soon realized these tools could help many others facing similar challenges. What started as a personal project quickly grew into a platform with thousands of daily users.

Today, our small team continues to develop and refine our collection of web tools with one goal in mind - to make digital tasks simpler and more accessible for everyone around the world.

Our Core Values

Simplicity

We believe the best tools are those you can use without reading a manual. We focus on creating intuitive interfaces that anyone can use immediately.

Accessibility

Our tools are designed to be accessible to everyone, regardless of technical expertise, device type, or location. We're committed to breaking down barriers.

Privacy

We respect your privacy. Our tools process data in your browser whenever possible, and we don't collect unnecessary personal information.
